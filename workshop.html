Workshops

Proposed by Team Vibhav

Team Vibhav organizes workshops on various trending topics every year to spread knowledge among all the students about electronics and different technologies and softwares that are in use. This Nimbus, Team Vibhav organizes two online workshops for everyone who have a zeal to learn .

Digital Workshop Processing

Digital signal processing (DSP) is the use of digital processing, such as by computers or more specialized digital signal processors, to perform a wide variety of signal processing operations. In this workshop various signal manipulation algorithms and build projects in Python will be taught. We will also tell about data visualization and analysis using various libraries in python.

WeBots

Webots is a professional mobile robot simulation software package. It offers a rapid prototyping environment for robots on professional level. This is a workshop organised by Team Vibhav on basics of robotics and how to simulate a robot in Webots software.
